About Hogwarts

Hogwarts was founded over a thousand years ago by Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin.

It is located in Scotland, hidden from the Muggle world by powerful enchantments.

The school is divided into four houses, each bearing the last name of one of its founders.

Students are sorted into houses upon their arrival at Hogwarts, and they compete for the House Cup throughout the school year.

Each house has its own common room and dormitories, and students are encouraged to take pride in their house.

Hogwarts also has a rich history of magical education, with a curriculum that includes subjects such as Potions, Transfiguration, and Defense Against the Dark Arts.

Students also participate in extracurricular activities, such as Quidditch, a popular wizarding sport played on broomsticks.

Houses

  • Slytherin: Known for ambition and cunning.
